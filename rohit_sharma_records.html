Scored 5 T20 International centuries — the most by any batsman globally. His 4 centuries came in just 12 matches in one period.

Career Milestones
2007
T20 World Cup debut & win
Part of India's inaugural T20 World Cup winning squad under MS Dhoni. Made his international debut.
2013
209 vs Australia — First double ton
Scored 209 against Australia in Bengaluru — first of his historic 5 ODI double centuries.
2014
World record 264 vs Sri Lanka
Set the world record for highest individual ODI score at Eden Gardens, Kolkata. A record that still stands.
2019
Test opener transformation + 5 WC tons
Promoted to Test opener; scored 529 in debut series. Also scored a record 5 centuries at the 2019 ODI World Cup in England.
2021
Appointed India's T20I captain
Named full-time T20I captain, later took over ODI and Test captaincy as well — India's captain across all formats.
2024
T20 World Cup champion & T20I retirement
Led India to the T20 World Cup title in Barbados, then announced retirement from T20 Internationals at the pinnacle.

IPL Records & Highlights
🏆
Most IPL titles as captain: 5
No other captain in IPL history has lifted the trophy 5 times. All 5 wins came with Mumbai Indians between 2013–2020.
💰
6000+ IPL runs — among all-time top scorers
One of only a handful of batters to cross 6000 runs in IPL history, alongside Virat Kohli, Shikhar Dhawan and David Warner.
💥
Highest IPL score: 109* vs KKR (2012)
Smashed an unbeaten 109 off 60 balls against Kolkata Knight Riders — his only IPL century and one of the finest in the tournament's history.
🎯
Loyal MI captain — 13+ seasons
Has captained Mumbai Indians for over 13 seasons, making him one of the longest-serving and most successful franchise captains in T20 cricket worldwide.
🌟
Orange Cap contender multiple seasons
Finished among the top run-scorers multiple times; was the second-highest scorer in IPL 2019 with 405 runs including 3 fifties.

Ranji Trophy & State Cricket
🏠
Mumbai — Ranji Trophy
Represents Mumbai in domestic cricket. Made his Ranji debut in 2006–07 and has been a consistent performer for the Mumbai side, known for match-winning knocks in crucial fixtures.
📋
309* — Highest First Class Score
Scored an unbeaten 309 in a Ranji Trophy match — one of the highest individual scores in Indian domestic cricket history, demonstrating his massive capacity for big innings.
🥇
Deodhar Trophy & Duleep Trophy standout
Represented West Zone in the Duleep Trophy and India A in the Deodhar Trophy, consistently scoring big runs that attracted national selectors' attention from an early age.
🌏
India A — Consistent performer
Made his mark in India A cricket before his international debut, earning a reputation as a technically sound, match-winning middle-order bat who could also open when needed.
